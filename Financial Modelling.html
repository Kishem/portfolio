Budget vs Actuals Dashboard (Variance Analysis)

It typically involves tracking expenses, revenues, or other financial metrics over a specific period and comparing them to the budget. Leveraged Buyout Model

An LBO (leveraged buyout) model is a financial tool used to assess the acquisition of a company primarily funded by borrowed money. It involves projecting cash flows, determining purchase prices, and structuring financing with a mix of debt and equity to evaluate potential returns and risks. This is typically done in Excel, which is what I will be using.

3 Statement Model

A 3 statement model integrates the income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement to provide a comprehensive view of a company's financial performance and position. It involves projecting future revenues, expenses, assets, liabilities, and cash flows to analyze the company's financial health, make strategic decisions, and forecast future performance.
